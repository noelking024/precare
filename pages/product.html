HRM Data Recorder

HRV data recorder is a non invasive powerful , multifunctional, single channel, Bluetooth enabled bio data logger that records the heart rate variability. The data is analyzed over the server with time and frequency domain analysis which is unique, patented and highly sensitive patented algorithm built by Dr. Ralf Arne Wittling. The hardware is designed and manufactured in Germany.

Evolution of ECG

Electrocardiogram (ECG) testing is now made simple. No more machines, papers and screens. All you need is the HRV Data Recorder and the software installed in your mobile.
